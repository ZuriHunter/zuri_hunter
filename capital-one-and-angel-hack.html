Capital One & AngelHack Small Biz Hackathon

0 comments Hackathon

Fifth Hackathon. Second AngelHack.

This weekend I have decided to take on the challenge again to enter a hackathon competition hosted by Capital One and Angel Hack. This is my first time going to a Capital One Hackathon, second time to an AngelHack hackahton and fifth time entering a hackathon. From each experience I improve and learn something new. The last hackathon I was a part of was Pearl Hacks that took place in Chapel Hill, Nort Carolina on UNC campus. There I finally learned how to succesfully implement the image upload gem Carrierwave for my Ruby On Rails application. I was able to enter my product into the competition sadly but I learned an awful lot and made a great connection with other female tech enthusiasts.

AngelHack was my very first hackathon that I attended. I came as a spectator to figure out what type of things people did there. So now coming to this event as a contributor is a truly a great marker of how far I have came along with my skill. At this event I will be a Developer with having Web Design and Ruby on Rails as my main skill set. What I hope to gain from this experience is of course expand my technology network but also learn how to implement two new gems and use an API to help deliver the idea.
